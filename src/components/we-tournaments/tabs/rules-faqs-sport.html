Frequently Ask Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I join the World Cup 2026 Sport Tournament?

Simply place qualifying bets on any World Cup matches through our supported sportsbook vendors (SABA, CMD368, SBOBET). You will automatically begin climbing the leaderboard based on your total turnover.

2. Can I see my current ranking?

Yes. Once logged in, you can check your current rank and score on the Leaderboard page. If your position is outside the Top 50, your rank and score will be shown as “-”.

3. What is the minimum bet to participate?

TThe minimum bet required for a wager to contribute to your tournament score is RM10 (or your local currency equivalent).

4. How often do the prizes change?

The prize pool varies over the 6-week period. Weeks 1–3 feature "Normal" prizes, Weeks 4–5 feature "Mid" prizes, and Week 6 offers the "Top Highest" prize.

5. When will I receive my prize if I win?

Prizes are distributed every Monday within 2 to 3 hours after the weekly tournament concludes.

6. Can I check who won in previous weeks?

Yes, the Leaderboard page allows all customers to view the winner history for the past 6 weeks.

7. How can I invite my friends to join?

You can use the Share button on the Leaderboard page to invite friends via WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, or by copying your unique referral link.

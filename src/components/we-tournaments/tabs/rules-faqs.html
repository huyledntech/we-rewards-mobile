Simply click Join Tournament and play any eligible Pragmatic Play slot games during the event period to accumulate points on the leaderboard.

Scores are based on your total wager amount on eligible games. Higher bets yield more points per spin.

When does the tournament start and end each week?

Tournaments run weekly from Monday 00:00 UTC to Sunday 23:59 UTC. Check the banner for exact timing.

How often are leaderboard rankings updated?

Leaderboard rankings are updated in real-time as players accumulate points throughout the tournament.

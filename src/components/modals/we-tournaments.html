How Scoring Works

Your score is based on your highest win multiplier.

Calculate Win multiplier:

Win Amount ÷ Bet Amount

Example: Bet MYR1 & Win MYR 50. Multiplier = 50x

Calculate Score:

Total win multiplier x 100

Example: Win Multiplier is 50, 50x100 = 5,000 score

The bigger your wins compared to your bet, the higher you score!
